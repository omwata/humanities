About

The School of Information Technology for Defence Systems, Humanities Department, is dedicated to providing KDF students with a comprehensive education in humanities. Our faculty are experts in their fields and are committed to helping students succeed. We offer a range of courses in the humanities, including Entrepreneurship, Research Methods, and IOM. Our programs are designed to prepare students for careers in academia, government, and the private sector. Please explore our website to learn more about our programs and Department, and feel free to contact us if you have any questions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide students with a high-quality education in the humanities that will prepare them for successful careers in academia, government, and the private sector. We are committed to helping students develop the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in their chosen field.

Our Faculty

Our faculty are experts in their fields and are dedicated to helping students succeed. They are committed to providing students with a comprehensive education in the humanities and are passionate about their work. Our Department are available to help students with any questions they may have and are committed to helping students achieve their goals.
