Academics

The School of Information Technology for Defence Systems, Humanities Department, offers a range of courses in humanities. Our faculty are experts in their fields and are dedicated to helping KDF students in technical field succeed.

Our Programs

Program Description
Entrepreneurship Learn skills for starting and running businesses: planning, marketing, and management.
Research Methods Develop skills in research design, data collection, analysis, and reporting.
Industrial Organisation Management Focus on managing organizations in the defence sector, covering HR and strategic management.
Communication Skills Enhance written, oral, and interpersonal communication for workplace success.
Principles and Practice of Managemengt Learn the principles and practices of management in the defence sector.
Business Plan Develop a business plan for a new venture, including marketing, financials, and operations.
Social Health Learn about social determinants of health and strategies for improving health outcomes.
